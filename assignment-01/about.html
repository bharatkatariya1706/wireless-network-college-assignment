About Us

The Institute of Engineering & Technology, DAVV commonly referred to as IET, or IET DAVV is the engineering school of Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya. It was founded as an autonomous institute in 1996. The college is located near the university's Takshashila Campus on Khandwa Road in the south-east of Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India.[2] It is an approved Institution by AICTE and University Grants Commission (UGC)


Acedemics

The institute offers various B.E. and M.E. programmes.[4] The institute started to offer B.Tech in Computer Science and Business System in partnership with TCS. The admission to B.E/B.Tech is through the JEE (Main) exam.
